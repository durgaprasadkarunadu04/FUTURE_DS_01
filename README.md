# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics
### Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics | Task 1 (2026)

---

## 📌 Objective
Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, 
high-value categories, and regional performance.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Python (Pandas)** – Data cleaning and exploratory analysis
- **Power BI** – Interactive dashboard creation

---

## 📁 Dataset
- **Source:** Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)
- **Size:** 9,994 rows × 21 columns
- **Columns used:** Order ID, Order Date, Category, Sub-Category, 
Product Name, Sales, Profit, Quantity, Region, Segment, and more

## 🔍 Key Insights
- **Technology** is the highest revenue category ($836K — 36% of total sales)
- **West region** leads with 31.58% of total sales
- **Canon ImageCLASS** is the top-selling product
- Sales **peak in November–December** — strong seasonal demand
- **Copiers** generate the highest profit among all sub-categories
- **Profit Margin** stands at 12.47%
- **Consumer segment** contributes the most to overall revenue
